Privacy Policy for Quark 2048

Last updated: July 4, 2026

QuarkVortexLabs ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by Quark 2048 ("the App"). This Privacy Policy applies solely to the App and its related services.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not actively collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information (PII) to our servers. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Google Play Games Services

The App integrates with Google Play Games Services to provide features such as global leaderboards. When you log in with your Google Play Games account, Google collects data in accordance with their privacy policy. We may receive your Google Play Games profile information (such as your username) and gameplay scores to display on leaderboards.

2. How We Use Information

Any information collected through third-party services is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's features (such as tracking your high scores and displaying leaderboards).
  • To monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your gaming experience.
  • To fix bugs and enhance performance.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Please review the privacy policies of these third-party service providers to understand how they handle your data:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Terms of Service

4. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us with any necessary information. We strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to take necessary actions.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
